About Grace Untamed

Hi, I’m Ashley Herring, a passionate graphic designer and Army veteran who believes in the power of design to create lasting impact. My approach blends strategy, creativity, and timeless aesthetics, ensuring that every brand I work with has a strong visual foundation that stands out. I specialize in branding, print, and digital design, with a particular focus on helping businesses—especially restaurants—craft cohesive, memorable brand identities.

For me, design is more than just making something look good—it’s about storytelling, connection, and purpose. I take a minimalist yet strategic approach, creating visuals that are not only eye-catching but also functional and meaningful. Whether it’s a logo that captures a brand’s essence or a brand identity system that reinforces trust and recognition, my goal is to design with impact and longevity in mind.

With an eye for detail and a dedication to timeless, effective design, I help brands build strong visual identities that leave a lasting impression. If you’re looking for a designer who can turn your ideas into something truly timeless, let’s create something great together.
